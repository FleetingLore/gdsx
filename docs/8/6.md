# 空间曲线的参数方程

- 名词 空间曲线的参数方程

$$ \left\{ \begin{matrix} x = x (t), \\ y = y (t), \\ z = z (t). \\ \end{matrix} \right. $$

- 名词 螺旋线

- 名词 螺距

- 曲面的参数方程
$$ \left\{ \begin{matrix} x = x (s, t), \\ y = y (s, t), \\ z = z (s, t). \\ \end{matrix} \right. $$
例如空间曲线 $\Gamma$
$$ \left\{ \begin{matrix} x = \varphi (t), \\ y = \psi (t),\\ z = \omega (t). \\ \end{matrix} \right. (\aplha \le t \le \beta) $$
绕 $z$ 轴旋转，得曲面
$$ \left\{ \begin{matrix} x = \sqrt{\left[\varphi (t)\right] ^2 + \left[\psi (t)\right] ^2} \cos \theta, \\ y = \sqrt{\left[\varphi (t)\right] ^2 + \left[\psi (t)\right] ^2} \sin \theta, \\ z = \omega (t). \\  \end{matrix} \right. \alpha \le t \le \beta, 0 \le \theta \le 2 \pi $$
